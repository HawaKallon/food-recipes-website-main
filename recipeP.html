Potato Leaves Recipe


Ingredients

  • 3 packs of already chopped sweet potato leaves
  • 200ml of Palm oil
  • Pack of Ogiri
  • Tin of Butter beans
  • 4 tablespoon Peanut butter
  • Assorted meat of your choice
  • Smoked fish
  • Scotch bonnet pepper (to your taste)
  • 2 medium sized Onions
  • Maggie seasoning (2 cubes)
  • salt (to your taste)
  • 3 Okra

Cooking Instructions

  1. Start with washing then bringing your meat in to boil until tender for approximately 30-40 minutes.

    2. Allow the meat to actually boil to tenderness

  2. Next wash the chopped potato leaves and leave it in a colander to drain any excess water.

    3. wash properly to remove gravel.

  3. Next blend onions and sscotch pepper with ogiri. This should look like a thick smooth paste.

    4. Make sure to blend properly

  4. After the meat has boiled, add the above paste mixture and your meat in a medium sized cooking pot, add 2 pints of water and bring to boil for about 20 minutes.

    5. Allow it to boil properly and do not add too much water at first.

  5. Whilst the pot boils, mix the Peanut butter with 50ml of water so it is slightly watery.

    6. Do not add too much water for it to be too light.

  6. Add Peanut mixture, palm oil, maggie seasoning and salt after the 20 minutes or until you have a thick soupy consistence.

    7. Mix well to actually spread the seasoning throughout

  7. Then add the washed potato leaves with the smoked fish. mix the pot and leave pot to boil for a further 15 mins in medium heat.

    8. Add the ingredients and allow to cook on a medium heat.

  8. Finally chop and crush okra and add to the boiling pot. Turn the heat low and let the sauce simmer for a further 5 mins or until the palm oil is floating.

    9. When the palm oil starts floating signifies that the dish is done and ready to be served.
    Enjoy your meal
