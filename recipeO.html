Okra Dish Recipe


Ingredients

  • 2 cups of fresh okra, sliced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup of palm oil
  • 1 cup of fish or meat stock
  • Salt and pepper to taste
