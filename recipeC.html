Crain-Crain Recipe


Ingredients

  • 2 bunch of crain crain
  • Palm oil
  • 3 medium sized onions
  • 6 scotch peppers or enough to your tolerance
  • 1 big pack of ogiri
  • A handful of okra
  • Meat (Kanda, cow, intestines)
  • Smoked fish
  • Bicarbonate soda
  • Maggie season
  • Salt

Cooking Instructions

  1. Pluck the leaves of the crain crain from the petiole (leaf stalk)

    2. Remove the leaves from the stems.

  2. Wash crain crain thoroughly and put aside

    3. Wash them properly to remove gravel.

  3. Wash and chop okra and put aside.

    4. Cut and put aside.

  4. Bring your meat, kanda (cow skin, intestines) to boil for about 40 minutes or as soft as you want it.

    5. Boil till tender.

  5. Meanwhile blend onions and scotch pepper with ogiri. This should look like a thick smooth paste.

    6. Blend til smooth.

  6. Add the above mixture to the boiled meat and add 2 pints of water.

    7. Add 2 pints of water.

  7. Add half a pint of palm oil to the pot and leave it to boil for 30 mins or until you have a thick soup consistency.

    8. Add 1/2 pint of palm oil.

  8. Add maggie season to taste. Then add 2 pinches of Bicarbonate soda

    9. Add maggie and Bicarbonate

  9. Once the stew starts foaming, add crain crain and okra and leave to cook for about 10 minutes. Leave the pot open Whilst pot boils and until the stew is cooked.

    10. Add the crain crain and okra

  10. Once the leaves are cooked, add fish and one or 2 scotch pepper for garnish when serving the dish later.

    11. Add the fish and pepper.
