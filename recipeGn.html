Sierra Leonean Stew Green Recipe


Ingredients

  • 3 cups of fresh green vegetables (e.g., spinach, kale)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup of groundnut paste (peanut paste)
  • 1/4 cup of palm oil
  • 1 cup of vegetable or meat broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste

  1. Heat palm oil in a pot over medium heat.

  6. Pour in vegetable or meat broth and bring to a simmer.
